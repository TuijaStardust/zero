Tove Jansson

Author ~ Illustrator ~ Painter ~ Cartoonist ~ Comic Strip Artist

The life of Tove Jansson

  • Born August 9th, 1914 in Helsinki, Finland
  • Wanted to become a painter
  • Studied art in Stockholm, Helsinki and Paris
  • Created cartoons for satirical magazine Garm in 1930s
  • First Moomin book – The Moomins and the Great Flood was published 1945
  • She published altogether 13 Moomin books
  • She published novels and collections of short stories
  • Painted a number of murals
  • Illustrated stories by other writers
  • Shared big part of her adult life with graphic artist Tuulikki Pietilä
  • Died 2001
